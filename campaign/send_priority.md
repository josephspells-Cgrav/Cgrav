## Send cadence recommendation

- **Mon:** Send Tier 1 (#1–#5). Direct owner emails, strongest observations.
- **Wed:** Send Tier 2 (#6–#10) after checking Mon replies.
- **Fri:** Send Tier 3 (#11–#14).
- **Mon+1:** Send Tier 4 (#15–#20). Lower personalization but still directly-published addresses.
- **Follow-ups:** Soft nudge on any Tier 1–2 without a reply after 5 business days. No new pitch, just a one-liner.

Keep daily send count ≤5 to stay under inbox-provider rate limits and preserve the "one per market" line's credibility.

## Pre-send checklist

1. Verify every address through NeverBounce / ZeroBounce / Hunter. Published ≠ deliverable.
2. For Gmail/Yahoo addresses (Philco, Full Spectrum, Valhalla, Freedom RWS, All American), expect stricter spam filtering — keep formatting plain, no tracking pixels on first send.
3. For generic `info@` and team addresses (Wide Meadow, Freedom, Mecklenburg, All American, Thor, Bumble), expect longer reply latency — the inbox isn't the owner's primary.
4. Rotate sending IP/domain if sending more than 10 in a single day.
