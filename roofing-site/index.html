08FAQ

The questions we always get.

Straight answers, no fine print. If yours isn't here, call us — we'll tell you the same thing over the phone.

How much does a roof replacement actually cost?
Charlotte-area replacements range from roughly $14,000 for a small architectural-shingle roof up to $38,000 for a large home with premium materials and specialty flashing. Your estimate is itemized line-by-line so you see exactly what's driving the number — and it's locked for 60 days.
Do I really need a new roof, or can I just repair it?
Depends on the damage pattern, age, and remaining shingle life. Our 38-point inspection tells you honestly. In the last twelve months we recommended repair (not replacement) for 31% of homeowners who called us — replacement isn't always the right answer, and we won't push it.
Will you work with my insurance company?
Yes, and we do it well. Our in-house licensed adjuster-liaison handles documentation, meets your carrier's adjuster on the roof, files supplements when they underpay, and has a 94% first-pass approval rate across 2,000+ claims. You pay your deductible — nothing else.
How long does the actual installation take?
A typical single-family home is 1–3 days. We start at sunrise, protect your landscaping and HVAC, and magnetically sweep the yard before we leave each evening. You can stay home the whole time — no need to relocate pets.
What happens if there's rain during installation?
We watch the radar hourly and only expose as much decking as we can dry-in that day. If weather moves in unexpectedly, we tarp immediately — we've never had a water-damage claim from a Peak installation. Your home stays dry, guaranteed in writing.
Are you licensed, bonded and insured?
Yes — NC GC license #77411, SC license #G121348. $2M general liability, $1M workers' comp per occurrence. We'll email certificates directly to you or your HOA before we even schedule the estimate.
What's the difference between your warranty and others?
Most "lifetime" warranties cover materials only and prorate after 10 years. Ours covers both materials (50 years, 25 non-prorated via GAF Golden Pledge) and our workmanship (lifetime, transferable once, in writing). If a shingle blows off in year 18, we replace it — parts and labor, free.
